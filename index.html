BIO

I really enjoyed the design aspect and would attempt a start up with a few fellow developers from Bioware. Well that failed within 6 months and I landed a job designing adn creating more practical items for ceremonies and events around Austin. I have been getting it done and learning everything I can do continue my journey as a designer.
